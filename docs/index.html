Territory Capturing Game

How to Play

  • Desktop: Use Arrow Keys to move
  • Mobile: Swipe in any direction to move
  • Objective: Fill 75% of the field
  • Strategy: Draw trails from filled areas (blue) into empty space (black)
  • Warning: Avoid bouncing balls and don't cross your own trail!
  • Q: Quit | R: Restart | Space: Next Level
